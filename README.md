# Hypas Law MDT — The Law Ledger

**A premium law-enforcement records system built for RedM and VORP.**

> Every arrest tells a story. Every case leaves a record. Hypas Law MDT keeps the frontier accountable.

Hypas Law MDT is designed for serious roleplay communities that need more than a basic report form. It brings operational records, people, charges, penal codes, fines, warrants, evidence, permissions and audit history into one structured and accountable interface.

## Core Features

### Records and Case Management

- Create and manage police reports
- Attach people, officers, charges and supporting information
- Archive and restore records
- Record last-edit information
- Lock sensitive records where authorised
- Maintain a searchable history of law-enforcement activity

### People and Officer Records

- Searchable person profiles
- Officer records and role information
- Linked reports, charges, warrants and fines
- Historical record visibility
- Structured case association

### Penal Codes and Charges

- Configurable penal-code catalogue
- Charge selection during report creation
- Fine and jail values
- Clear offence descriptions
- Consistent sentencing information

### Warrants, Fines and Jail

- Warrant management
- Fine and law-bill workflows
- Jail-sentence handling
- Approval controls for higher-value enforcement actions
- Government-fund integration where configured
- Citizen fine access through supported player workflows

### Evidence and Auditability

- Evidence-linked case records
- Search and activity history
- Administrative audit trail
- Record locking and controlled editing
- Archive and restoration tracking
- Clear accountability for staff actions

### Rank Permissions

- Rank-based access control
- Configurable permissions by role
- Controlled access to sensitive actions
- Dedicated permissions interface
- Separation between viewing, editing and administrative authority

### Interface

- Midnight Frontier presentation
- Premium western court-ledger styling
- Structured navigation
- Searchable tables and records
- Readable penal-code and audit views
- Consistent contrast and scalable interface controls

## Intended Use

Hypas Law MDT is intended for:

- RedM police departments
- Sheriff and marshal offices
- Federal or territorial law agencies
- Court and justice roleplay
- VORP servers requiring persistent law-enforcement records
- Communities that need rank permissions and audit accountability

## Requirements

The final release documentation will identify exact supported versions. The expected core environment is:

- RedM
- VORP Core
- `oxmysql`
- A supported MySQL or MariaDB database
- Required Hypas bridge or integration files included with the customer package

Hypas Law MDT is designed as a standalone MDT product and does not require Hypas Law unless a specific optional integration is documented.

Always use the installation documentation supplied with the purchased release.
